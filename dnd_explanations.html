Dungeons & Dragons terms

  Navigation  













RPG = Role-playing game

TTRPG = Tabletop role-playing game

d6 = 6-sided die. the dX means that the die has X amount of sides, like d20.

Rule of cool = When the DM rules that something works or happens even though it would not make sense in the story setting or is not RAW

RAW = Rules As Written are the formal rules, there is also RAI = Rules as Intented, when there might have been a slip by the creator of the RPG

DnD = Dungeons & Dragons, the pen and paper RPG

Campaign = A game that lasts for a long time and is played in many sessions, our first campaign Storm King's Thunder lasted almost two years

One-shot = a game that isn't as long as a campaign, usually the setting lasts for one to two sessions of 2-6 hours, or a really long one session
